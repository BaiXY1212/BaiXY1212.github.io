Sensory Overload: My Experience at TeamLab

November 23, 2025

Last weekend, I visited the TeamLab exhibition. It wasn’t just a visual spectacle; it was a profound lesson on interaction.

(Image source: Unsplash example)

The Power of Immersion#

As a Data Science student, I often look at numbers. But walking through the “Crystal Universe,” I realized that data, when visualized correctly, becomes an experience.

The lights didn’t just blink; they reacted to my presence. This made me think about my current research on museum accessibility:

How can we translate this kind of visual awe into sound or touch for the visually impaired?

Technology should not just be smart; it should be emotional. This exhibition solidified my desire to work at the intersection of HCI and AI—to build tools that don’t just solve problems but also create joy.

What’s Next?#

I plan to replicate a small-scale version of this interactive lighting using Python and Arduino in my next semester’s project. Stay tuned!
